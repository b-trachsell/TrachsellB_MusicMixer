Welcome To Dog Orchestra

MMED-1055
Music Mixer
Breanna Trachsell

These dogs have been practicing for a month straight for this performance. They are some of the best and brightest dogs of their generation.

They have all remarkably perfected their individual parts but due to social distancing, were never able to practice together.

Until Today.

Please do forgive them if they do not play in sync, they are merely dogs you see; and are trying their best

Drag each dog you want to hear play to a spot on the stage. Either drag them back down or hit the pause button in the top right to have them stop.

Hit the R button to send everyone back to the floor and begin a new composition

Mix and Match the sounds to make your own composition!
